About

Melbourne International Sunny Arts Profile: Melbourne International Sunny Arts was founded in 2011 and registered in 2014. It is composed of a group of people who love culture and art.

Purpose: Through professional training, let the students because of the charm of music have a deep soul resonance, love each other, the heart is connected, let people feel warmth and hope. The Sunny Arts mainly introduces Chinese culture and art to the multi-culture of Australia by holding various large and small concerts, and hopes to learn from and communicate with the multi-culture. Live together and create a future with tolerance in Australia's multicultural atmosphere.

Jennifer Wang, founder of Melbourne International Sunny Arts Ensemble Association Inc.

Jennifer Wang, who graduated from the Department of Vocal Music of Tianjin Conservatory of Music in 1989, has been working as a soprano (lyric soprano) in Tianjin Song and Dance Theater for 10 years.

During the study time at the Conservatory of Music, She participated in the concert of young foreign artists and served as the lead singer of the fourth movement of Beethoven's Ninth Symphony "Ode to Joy", conducted by the American conductor Mr. BAKSTER. After graduation, She became a singer of Tianjin Song and Dance Academy and participated in many musicals, large-scale concerts and local performances.

In 1996, Jennifer Wang was a vocal singing technical immigrant to Australia with the special approval of the Australian government, and has founded the Melbourne International Sunny Arts , the Melbourne Chinese Philharmonic Chamber Orchestra, and the Victoria Arts Federation, which has cultivated a large number of singing talents over the years.

Through decades of music performance experience, Jennifer Wang has met many simple and kind people and discovered that music is the temple of human spiritual growth. As a professional singer, it is her responsibility to teach vocal singing skills. In 2011, she founded Melbourne Sunny Arts, which has prepared professional vocal training opportunities for many music lovers. Hundreds of students have participated in professional systematic singing skills training to meet the best of themselves. A variety of social events and music events, involving tens of thousands of people and affecting tens of thousands of people. To make their own modest contribution to the multi-cultural and artistic exchange in Australia.

In 2011, she founded the Melbourne International Sunny Arts Ensemble(hereinafter referred to as Sunny Arts), and served as the voice method training, solfeggio ear training, chorus conductor and other work for free.
With unremitting efforts and the participation of its members, Sunny Choir gradually developed. We have been invited to participate in various public welfare activities in the local community, including visiting nursing homes, recording programmes for radio stations, donating to charity for the Royal Children's Hospital in Melbourne, Australia Day celebrations and Chinese New Year celebrations.

In 2012, she led the students of the choir to participate in the first Chinese Spring Festival Gala held in Melbourne, and all the members of the Sunny Choir performed the grand finale chorus "Unforgettable Tonight".

In 2013, she led hundreds of members of the Sunny Arts to participate in the celebration and performance activities of Australia Day, learning from different ethnic groups in a multicultural environment.

In 2015,Jennifer Wang planned, prepared, organized and directed the "2015 Melbourne International Sunny Arts Spring Festival Gala" held at the Grand Theatre of Melbourne City Hall. By combining professional artists from home and abroad with resources and professional actors from Australia, the gala was an unprecedented success and enriched the diverse culture and art of Australia, thus affecting the attention of tens of thousands of people at home and abroad. In 2015, "Melbourne Chinese New Year Gala" in the more than 2000 people's City Hall theatre was full, and one ticket was difficult to obtain.

Before and after the 2015 Spring Festival Gala performance, she also organized two "vocal master classes", one of which was jointly organized with the "Melbourne Music Club", so that all vocal music lovers could participate. "Vocal Master Class" is personally guided by domestic vocal professors, local and even Sydney vocal lovers signed up to participate in the training, everyone said that they benefited a lot, and look forward to having more opportunities to get expert professors.

In 2015, in Recital Centre, participated in the "70th Anniversary of the Victory of the Anti-Fascist War" performed by Chinese and foreign musicians, more than 1,000 people participated in the large-scale musical evening, as a member of the "Yellow River Chorus" composed of more than 70 people rehearsing and conducting work, achieved unprecedented success! The performance was reported on Phoenix TV.
